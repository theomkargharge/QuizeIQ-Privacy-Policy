1. Introduction

Welcome to QuizeIQ ("we," "us," "our," or "Company"). At QuizeIQ, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience on our application. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding the collection, use, disclosure, and safeguarding of your information when you use our mobile application.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By accessing and using QuizeIQ, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by all the terms of this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when you use QuizeIQ, including:

  • Account Information: Email address, password, name, profile picture, and other account registration details
  • Quiz Performance Data: Your quiz scores, answers, completion times, and progress through quizzes
  • User Preferences: Your learning preferences, notification settings, and app customization choices
  • Communication Data: Any messages, feedback, or support requests you send to us

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use QuizeIQ, certain information is automatically collected:

  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers, mobile network information, and device settings
  • Usage Data: App features used, time and duration of use, pages visited, and interactions within the app
  • Location Information: General location data (country/region) derived from IP address for analytics purposes
  • Crash and Error Logs: Information about app crashes, errors, and performance issues (collected by Firebase Crashlytics)
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: We use analytics cookies and similar tracking technologies to understand user behavior

2.3 Information from Third Parties

We may receive information about you from:

  • Firebase Services: Authentication and analytics data from Firebase platform services
  • Analytics Providers: Aggregate usage patterns and behavior analytics

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

3.1 Core Service Delivery

  • Creating and maintaining your user account

3.3 Analytics and Improvements

  • Analyzing user behavior to improve app functionality and user experience
  • Monitoring app performance and identifying technical issues
  • Conducting research and analytics to understand user preferences
  • Tracking crash data and error reports to enhance stability
  • Measuring engagement and creating usage statistics

3.4 Security and Legal Compliance

  • Protecting against fraud, abuse, and other harmful activities
  • Complying with legal obligations and regulatory requirements
  • Enforcing our Terms of Service and other agreements
  • Investigating security incidents and suspicious activities

3.5 Marketing and Business Purposes

  • Sending promotional content and newsletters (with your consent)
  • Creating aggregated, de-identified statistical data
  • Conducting surveys and gathering feedback

4. Third-Party Services and Data Sharing

Important: We share your information with the following third-party service providers to enable core app functionality. These services are bound by confidentiality agreements and data processing agreements.

4.1 Firebase Services

Google Firebase is our primary backend service provider. We use:

  • Firebase Authentication: For secure user login and account management
  • Cloud Firestore: For storing user data, quiz results, and progress information
  • Firebase Realtime Database: For real-time data synchronization
  • Firebase Crashlytics: For monitoring app crashes and error reporting
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM): For sending push notifications

Firebase Privacy Policy | Google Privacy Policy

4.2 Analytics Providers

We may use analytics tools integrated through Firebase Analytics to understand app usage patterns and user behavior. This data is anonymized and aggregated.

4.3 Data Sharing Restrictions

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal data to third parties
  • Share personal information for marketing purposes without your explicit consent
  • Disclose personal information to unaffiliated third parties, except as required by law
  • Use your quiz answers or personal details for unauthorized purposes

4.4 Legal Disclosure

We may disclose your information when required by law, such as in response to a subpoena, court order, or government request. We will attempt to notify you of such requests when legally permissible.

5. Data Security

5.1 Security Measures

We implement comprehensive technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption: Data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using SSL/TLS protocols
  • Secure Authentication: Firebase Authentication uses industry-standard security practices
  • Access Control: Only authorized personnel have access to personal data
  • Regular Security Audits: We conduct periodic security reviews and penetration testing
  • Firebase Security: We leverage Google's enterprise-grade security infrastructure

5.2 Limitations

While we implement strong security measures, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security, and any transmission is at your own risk. You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your login credentials.

5.3 Password Management

  • Never share your password with others
  • Use a strong, unique password for your account
  • Change your password regularly
  • Log out after using the app, especially on shared devices

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Portability

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data stored in your account
  • Download a copy of your quiz results and user data
  • Obtain information about what data we collect and how it is used

6.2 Modification and Deletion

  • Update Information: You can update your account details directly through the app settings
  • Delete Account: You can delete your account by contacting our support team
  • Data Deletion: Upon account deletion, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except as required by law

6.3 Opt-Out Options

  • Notifications: You can disable push notifications in your device settings and app preferences
  • Marketing Communications: You can opt out of promotional emails by following the unsubscribe link in emails
  • Analytics: You may opt out of analytics tracking through your device settings

6.4 Regional Rights

GDPR (EU Users): If you are in the European Union, you have additional rights under GDPR, including the right to erasure, right to rectification, and right to object to processing.

CCPA (California Users): If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act, including the right to know, delete, and opt-out.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email address provided in the Contact section.

7. Data Retention

7.1 Retention Periods

  • Active Account Data: While your account is active, we retain all your personal data and quiz results
  • Deleted Accounts: After deletion, personal data is purged within 30 days (some data may be retained for legal compliance)
  • Crash Logs: Error and crash data is retained for 90 days for analytics purposes
  • Backup Data: Backup copies may be retained for up to 90 days

7.2 Legal Holds

We may retain data longer than stated above if required by law, for litigation purposes, or to comply with legal obligations.

Your continued use of QuizeIQ after such modifications constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
